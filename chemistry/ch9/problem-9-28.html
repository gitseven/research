Coordination Compounds · Problem 9.28

Original Question

The number of ions produced from the complex Co(NH₃)₆Cl₃ in solution is: (i) 6 (ii) 4 (iii) 3 (iv) 2

Background Concept — Inner vs Outer Sphere

In Werner-type complexes, ligands inside the coordination sphere are non‑ionisable in solution, whereas counter‑ions (outside the sphere) dissociate. In Co(NH₃)₆Cl₃, all six NH₃ ligands are inner‑sphere; the three chloride ions are outside as counter‑ions. Upon dissolution, the complex cation [Co(NH₃)₆]³⁺ and three Cl⁻ anions are produced, yielding a total of four ions per formula unit.

Answer

Co(NH₃)₆Cl₃ → [Co(NH₃)₆]³⁺ + 3Cl⁻ → total ions = 4 → (ii)
