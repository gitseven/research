Chapter 7 · Problem 2

Question

Find pressure at depth 10 m in water. (ρ_water = 1000 kg/m³, g = 9.8 m/s²)

Background Concept — Fluid Pressure

Pressure in fluids increases with depth due to weight of fluid above. P = P₀ + ρgh where P₀ is atmospheric pressure, ρ is density, g is acceleration due to gravity, and h is depth. This is hydrostatic pressure.

Solution

P = P₀ + ρgh = 1.01×10⁵ + (1000)(9.8)(10) = 1.01×10⁵ + 9.8×10⁴ = 1.01×10⁵ + 0.98×10⁵ = 1.99×10⁵ Pa

Tips to Solve

  • Use P = P₀ + ρgh
  • Add atmospheric pressure
  • Check units (Pa)
  • Verify with known values
  • Consider depth units

Key Formulas

P = P₀ + ρgh
