Chapter 6 · Problem 3

Question

A 2 kg mass is moved from Earth's surface to height 3R above surface. Find change in gravitational potential energy.

Background Concept — Gravitational Potential Energy

Gravitational potential energy U = -GMm/r is negative because work is done against gravity. The reference point is at infinity where U = 0. Change in PE = U_final - U_initial. Work done equals negative change in PE.

Solution

U_surface = -GMm/R. U_3R = -GMm/(4R). ΔU = U_3R - U_surface = -GMm/(4R) - (-GMm/R) = -GMm/(4R) + GMm/R = 3GMm/(4R) = 3mg₀R/4 = 3(2)(9.8)(6.37×10⁶)/4 = 9.37×10⁷ J

Tips to Solve

  • Use U = -GMm/r
  • Calculate at both positions
  • Find difference
  • Use g₀ = GM/R²
  • Check units (J)

Key Formulas

U = -GMm/r, ΔU = U_final - U_initial
