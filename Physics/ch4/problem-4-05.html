Chapter 4 · Problem 5

Question

A 2 kg ball is dropped from 10 m height. Find speed just before hitting ground.

Background Concept — Conservation of Energy

When only conservative forces act, total mechanical energy is conserved. KE + PE = constant. Initial PE converts to final KE.

Solution

Initial: PE = mgh = (2)(9.8)(10) = 196 J, KE = 0. Final: PE = 0, KE = ½mv². Conservation: 196 = ½(2)v². v² = 196. v = 14 m/s

Tips to Solve

  • Apply energy conservation
  • Set initial = final
  • Choose reference level
  • Check units
  • Verify with kinematics

Key Formulas

KE + PE = constant
