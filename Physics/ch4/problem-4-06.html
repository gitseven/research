Chapter 4 · Problem 6

Question

A motor lifts 100 kg mass 5 m in 10 seconds. Calculate power.

Background Concept — Power

Power is rate of doing work. P = W/t = Fd/t = Fv. It's measured in watts (W) or horsepower. Average power = total work/time.

Solution

W = mgh = (100)(9.8)(5) = 4900 J. P = W/t = 4900/10 = 490 W

Tips to Solve

  • Calculate work first
  • Use P = W/t
  • Check units
  • Consider time
  • Verify with P = Fv

Key Formulas

P = W/t = Fv
